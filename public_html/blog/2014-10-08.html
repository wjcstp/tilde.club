2014.10.8 tilde crash

It's interesting that i was followed by a couple of fellow tildenizens on twitter during the outage, and i followed some too. It's like we needed the break from the site itself to connect other ways, and also a way to stay on top of some of the community; otherwise impossible with now 615 members (though not all active yet).
